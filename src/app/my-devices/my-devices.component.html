Door To Open your door

By clicking on "Open my door", a transaction with "Open my door" as the message is created. The miners then participate in Proof of Elapsed Time (PoET). The winner of PoET will mine the block. Your door will open in few seconds.

Light To On your light

By clicking on "On my light", a transaction with "On my light" as the message is created. The miners then participate in Proof of Elapsed Time (PoET). The winner of PoET will mine the block. Your light will on in few seconds.
